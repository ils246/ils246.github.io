Searching for Scientific Revolutions

Citation Analysis

In order to begin our search for paradigm shifts in science, we being by understanding the average system behavior for citation patterns.

First, we explore the dependance on previous knowledge for the knowledge output of a particular year. We do this by plotting the distribution of the publication year of all the references in our corpus from the last five years.

We plot the raw counts and the densities to correct for citation inflation and the increase of papers publoshed per year.
