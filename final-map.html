What is the Referendum Illustrator?

In October 2016, Colombians were asked to endorse or reject thourgh popular vote the peace agreement between the Farc and the
Colombian government. After aproximately four years and 38 rounds of negotiations, this peace agreement promised to put an end
to 51 years of the national conflict - one of the longest standing in Latin America. Nationals expected that the agreement would be
ratified in order to start working towards the a long desired peace. However, the country was shocked when the results revealed that
the opposition had won the vote by 0.2%. Some have considered this result to be comparable to the other democratic anomalies that
occurred in 2016. The referendum illustrator enables the interactive visalization of the referendum results and their comparison against
other demographic data at the department level.
